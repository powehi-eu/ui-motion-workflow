# MagicUI Provider

## Role

`magicui` is the product-friendly component provider in the reference stack.

Use it when the UI needs polished animated components that still feel structured and system-compatible.

## Best Fit

- cards
- feature sections
- navigation
- section-level UI blocks
- product-marketing hybrid surfaces

## Strengths

- more component-oriented than effect-oriented
- easier to fit into modern Tailwind and TypeScript stacks
- good middle ground between motion and structure

## Risks

- may feel less distinctive than more expressive providers when the brief needs a real showcase moment

## Prefer It When

- the surface is product-facing
- the team wants polish without theatrics
- the page already has clear hierarchy and needs stronger component quality

## Avoid It When

- the brief explicitly calls for ambient, cinematic, or highly expressive motion
- only a subtle motion layer is missing inside an already correct local component
